You are preparing for the biggest day of sales for the year, where your traffic will increase by 100x. You have already setup SQS standard queue. What should you do?

Explicación

Amazon SQS leverages the AWS cloud to dynamically scale based on demand. SQS scales elastically with your application so you don't have to worry about capacity planning and pre-provisioning. There is no limit to the number of messages per queue, and standard queues provide nearly unlimited throughput. Read more: https://aws.amazon.com/sqs/
